# The Section 199A Deduction in 2021: Planning While the Pass-Through Window Is Still Open

**Originally published:** November 2021  
**Category:** Tax Planning

## Summary

With only a few years remaining before the scheduled sunset of Section 199A, 2021 is a good time for pass-through owners to review whether they are capturing the deduction efficiently.

## What owners often miss

The deduction is not just about taxable income level. Compensation design, activity aggregation, W-2 wages, and qualified property can all affect the outcome.

## Planning themes

- review S corporation compensation levels
- revisit aggregation elections and grouping logic
- evaluate whether ownership structure still supports the intended deduction

## Bottom line

The most effective Section 199A work is proactive. Owners who wait until filing season often reduce the number of levers still available.
