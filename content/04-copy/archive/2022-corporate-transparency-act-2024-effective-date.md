# The Corporate Transparency Act's Beneficial Ownership Rules: Preparing Your Business for the 2024 Effective Date

**Originally published:** October 2022  
**Category:** Entity Structure

## Summary

FinCEN's 2022 final rule made it clear that beneficial ownership reporting was moving from concept to operational requirement. The businesses that benefit most from early preparation are usually the ones least likely to have centralized compliance systems.

## Why the lead time matters

This regime depends on legal-entity maps, ownership records, and control analysis. Those are rarely assembled well under deadline pressure, especially for businesses with layered ownership or multiple legacy entities.

## Questions to answer early

- which entities are likely reporting companies
- whether an exemption applies and why
- who exercises substantial control
- who will own the filing process internally

## Bottom line

The smartest move in 2022 is not filing. It is building the entity and ownership record required to file accurately in 2024.
