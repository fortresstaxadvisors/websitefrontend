# About

Fortress Tax Advisors was built to provide the kind of tax relationship serious clients actually need: measured, highly responsive, technically grounded, and capable of supporting decisions beyond filing season.

Over the last five years, Fortress has focused on building a body of work around business tax strategy, trust and estate tax, multi-state complexity, transaction planning, and advisor coordination. The firm is designed to create clarity early, reduce execution friction, and support clients in environments where tax decisions affect real outcomes.
